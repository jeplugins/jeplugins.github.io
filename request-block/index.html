Pro Features

Take a closer look at the Pro features that make managing feedback effortless.

6 Color Themes

Choose from 6 beautiful pre-designed themes: Default (Indigo), Ocean (Cyan), Forest (Green), Sunset (Orange), Purple (Violet), and Minimal (Gray). You can also create your own custom color with the color picker.

Custom Statuses

Create your own workflow with custom statuses. Add statuses like "Under Review", "Needs Info", or "Scheduled" — whatever fits your process. Each status gets its own color.

Admin Moderation

Keep your request board clean and professional. When moderation is enabled, all new submissions go to a "Pending Review" queue. You decide what gets published.

Email Notifications

Keep your users in the loop automatically. When you update a request status, users receive a notification email. Show users you're listening and building what they asked for.
